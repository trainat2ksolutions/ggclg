About the College

Government Girls P.U. College, Jamkhandi is a premier institution dedicated to the empowerment of women through education. Offering Arts, Commerce, and Science streams, the college strives to develop academic excellence, moral values, and social responsibility among students.

Courses Offered

We provide a wide range of subjects under Arts, Commerce, and Science streams to help students pursue their career goals.

Arts Stream

Subjects include History, Political Science, Sociology, Economics, and Kannada with other Arts Skills

Commerce Stream

Subjects include Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Statistics .

Science Stream

Subjects include Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics to strengthen analytical and technical skills.
